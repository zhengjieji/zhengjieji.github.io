Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Virginia Tech under the supervision of Prof. Peng Gao. I got my M.S. degree in Machine Learning from KTH Royal Institute of Technology in 2022. I received my B.Eng. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2021.

My research interest lies in security, systems and machine learning. My current research focuses on system security, cyber threat intelligence, and large language model for cyber threat protection.

I have devoted myself in animal rescue since 2017. The goal is to save and protect animals from suffering around the world. I worked as the president of Shanghai Jiao Tong University Small Animal Protection Association (SJTUSAPA) from 2018 - 2020.
